VIBE CHECKER

🤔 What is Vibe Checker?

It's a web app that interprets the emotion expressed in the comments, and it can be used to help online creators determine how their audience feels about the subject being shown — whether it be an image, video, post or article. Each comment has an emoji badge that corresponds to the vibe that the comment emits (hopefully they are good vibes 😎).

👩‍💻 Made by Tengku Hana Shafikah during the Spring 2020 Google Software Product Sprint.
